## Phase 1: Critical (Week 1) — On-Page & Headline Foundation

**Goal:** Fix structure and hero so the page is technically correct and instantly clear.  
**Outcome:** On-Page SEO and H1 Headline moving toward 10/10.

### 1.1 H1 & Heading Structure (→ 10/10 H1 Headline)

- [ ] **Single H1:** Audit the page and ensure only ONE `<h1>` exists (the hero headline). Change all other section titles to `<h2>` or `<h3>` with a proper hierarchy (no skipping levels).
- [ ] **Rewrite H1:** Replace current H1 with an outcome-focused line, e.g.  
  **"We build the AI-powered software that scales your business"**  
  (≤12 words, clear, benefit-driven.)
- [ ] **Hero subheadline:** Add outcome-focused subheadline, e.g.  
  *"From AI agents to full SaaS platforms—we take your product from idea to launch in weeks, not months. 50+ projects shipped."*

### 1.2 Meta Tags (→ 10/10 On-Page SEO)

- [ ] **Meta title:**  
  - Max **60 characters**.  
  - Example: `Tech Emulsion | AI Software Development Agency`
- [ ] **Meta description:**  
  - Max **155 characters**.  
  - Example: `We build AI agents, SaaS platforms, and custom software. 50+ projects shipped. Get a free estimate.`

### 1.3 CTA Unification (→ 10/10 Primary CTA)

- [ ] Replace all primary CTAs with **one** consistent line: **"Get a Free Estimate"** (or "Get a Free Project Estimate").
- [ ] Update: nav CTA, floating button, hero CTA, and bottom form primary button so they all use the same label and intent.
- [ ] Remove or secondary-style: "Book a Discovery Call", "Talk to Sales", "Get a Quote", "Send Message" as primary actions.

### 1.4 Heading Hierarchy (On-Page SEO)

- [ ] Map all headings: one H1 (hero), then H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections. No multiple H1s, no skipped levels (e.g. H2 → H4).

**Phase 1 check:** One H1, correct meta length, one primary CTA, clean hierarchy.

---

## Phase 2: High Priority (Weeks 2–3) — Copy, Trust, Technical SEO

**Goal:** Human-focused copy, strong trust signals, and better technical SEO.  
**Outcome:** Supporting Copy, Social Proof, and Technical SEO moving toward 10/10.

### 2.1 Supporting Copy & Messaging (→ 10/10)

- [ ] **Perspective shift:** Rewrite sections from "We" to "You" (e.g. "You get", "Your team gets", "Your product ships").
- [ ] **Remove buzzwords:** Replace "cutting-edge solutions", "innovative designs", "impactful digital experiences" with concrete outcomes and proof.
- [ ] **Add specifics:** Where relevant, add engagement hints: pricing ranges or "from $X", typical timelines (e.g. "weeks not months"), team size (e.g. "dedicated pod of 3–5").
- [ ] **Service copy:** Rewrite keyword-stuffed lines (e.g. "Best rated AI generative expand solutions") into clear, grammatical, benefit-led descriptions.

### 2.2 Social Proof & Trust (→ 10/10)

- [ ] **Testimonials:** Add **5–8** client testimonials with: full name, role, company name, and headshot (or company logo).
- [ ] **Verifiable metrics:** Replace unverifiable "100% satisfaction" with: link to Clutch/G2/Upwork, JSS score, or specific KPIs (e.g. "N% faster delivery", "X projects delivered").
- [ ] **Portfolio:** Add quantified results to case studies (timeline, funding, users, revenue impact, etc.).
- [ ] **External proof:** Get listed on **Clutch.co** and embed the Clutch widget; add link to **Upwork** profile and highlight JSS + review count.

### 2.3 Technical SEO (→ 10/10)

- [ ] **Schema:**  
  - Keep Organization schema.  
  - Add **FAQ** schema to the existing FAQ section.  
  - Add **Service** schema for main offerings (can be on homepage or later on service pages).
- [ ] **Canonical:** Keep canonical tag; ensure it’s correct on all new pages.
- [ ] **Images:**  
  - Keep alt text on all images (maintain 0 missing alts).  
  - Introduce **lazy loading** for below-the-fold images.  
  - Convert key images to **WebP** and **compress** (especially portfolio) to reduce page weight and improve LCP.

**Phase 2 check:** Copy is you-focused and specific; 5–8 testimonials and verifiable proof; FAQ + Service schema; faster, lighter images.

---

## Phase 3: Content & Authority (Weeks 4–8) — 10/10 Content Strategy

**Goal:** Topical authority, no keyword cannibalization, and a clear content engine.  
**Outcome:** Content Strategy for SEO at 10/10.

### 3.1 Dedicated Service Pages

- [ ] Create **dedicated service pages** (e.g. `/services/ai-engineering`, `/services/saas-development`, `/services/mobile-apps`) with **1,500+ words** each.
- [ ] Each page: one H1, clear H2/H3 structure, benefit-led copy, and a single primary CTA (e.g. "Get a Free Estimate").
- [ ] Add **Service** schema on each service page.
- [ ] Move detailed service copy from homepage to these pages to fix **keyword cannibalization** and target distinct keywords per page.

### 3.2 Blog & Long-Tail Strategy

- [ ] **Cadence:** Publish **2–4 posts per month** targeting long-tail keywords (e.g. "how to build an AI agent for customer support", "SaaS MVP development timeline").
- [ ] **Internal linking:** Link from blog posts to relevant service pages and homepage; link from service pages to related posts.
- [ ] **Content themes:** Align with service pillars (AI agents, SaaS, mobile, custom software) to build topical authority.

### 3.3 Backlinks & Authority

- [ ] **Backlink strategy:** Define 5–10 target link sources (industry directories, partner sites, guest posts, tool/product listings).
- [ ] Add to **relevant directories** and **Clutch/G2**; encourage client reviews and case study co-promotion.
- [ ] Optional: simple **linkable assets** (e.g. "State of AI Development 2026", templates, checklists) to attract links.

**Phase 3 check:** Service pages live with Service schema; blog running 2–4 posts/month; backlink plan in motion.

---

## Phase 4: Polish & Sustain (Ongoing) — Maintain 10/10

**Goal:** Keep all categories at 10/10 and improve Core Web Vitals and UX.

### 4.1 Performance & Core Web Vitals

- [ ] **Page weight:** Reduce total image count or lazy-load/compress so that LCP and TTI stay in the green (aim for <76 heavy images on a single page; consider splitting or loading on scroll).
- [ ] **Critical path:** Minimize render-blocking resources; defer non-critical JS/CSS.
- [ ] Monitor **Core Web Vitals** (LCP, FID/INP, CLS) in Search Console and fix regressions.

### 4.2 Copy & CTA Consistency

- [ ] Any new page or section: single H1, you-focused copy, one primary CTA ("Get a Free Estimate").
- [ ] Annual (or bi-annual) copy audit to remove new buzzwords and keep specifics (pricing, timelines, proof).

### 4.3 Content & Schema Maintenance

- [ ] New service or major offering → dedicated page + Service schema.
- [ ] New FAQ blocks → FAQ schema.
- [ ] Keep meta title ≤60 chars and meta description ≤155 chars on all key pages.
